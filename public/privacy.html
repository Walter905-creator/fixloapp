Fixlo Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone number, address
  • Professional Information: Business details, licenses, certifications (for contractors)
  • Job Requests: Service details, location, project descriptions
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we don't store card details)
  • Device Information: Push notification tokens, device type, app version

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Connect homeowners with qualified professionals
  • Send push notifications about job matches and updates
  • Process subscription payments
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our app and services
  • Ensure safety and prevent fraud

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only when:

  • With Professionals: We share homeowner job requests with relevant contractors
  • With Homeowners: We share professional contact info when matched
  • Service Providers: Stripe for payments, Expo for push notifications
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect safety

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption and secure servers.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access, update, or delete your account information
  • Opt out of push notifications
  • Cancel your subscription at any time
  • Request data export or deletion

6. Push Notifications

We use Expo Push Notifications to send you:

  • New job opportunities (for professionals)
  • Updates on your requests (for homeowners)
  • Important account notifications

You can disable notifications in your device settings.

7. Subscription and Payments

Professional subscriptions are processed through Stripe. Subscriptions automatically renew monthly at $59.99 unless cancelled.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18. We don't knowingly collect information from children under 18.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. We'll notify you of significant changes through the app or email.

10. Contact Us

For privacy questions, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@fixloapp.com
  • Website: fixloapp.com/contact
