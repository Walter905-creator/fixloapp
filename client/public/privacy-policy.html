Fixlo Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 22, 2025

Fixlo ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Fixlo mobile application and website (https://www.fixloapp.com). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your data when you use our services.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, and address (for service requests)
  • Professional Information: Trade type, service area, and background check data (for pros)
  • Device Information: Device ID, IP address, and operating system
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by Stripe (we do not store card details)
  • Communication Data: SMS, email, or push notification messages

How We Use Your Information

  • Match homeowners with professionals near them
  • Process payments and subscriptions
  • Send job notifications via SMS and push messages
  • Improve app functionality and customer experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

Sharing Your Information

We only share information with service providers (Stripe, Twilio, Checkr), professional users when a homeowner submits a request, and legal authorities when required by law. We do not sell or rent your data.

SMS & Communication Consent

By opting in during signup, you agree to receive SMS messages related to job leads, service requests, or account updates. You may opt out anytime by replying "STOP".

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal requirements. You may request deletion anytime by emailing pro4u.improvements@gmail.com.

Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and access control to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, and you use Fixlo at your own risk.

Children's Privacy

Fixlo is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at pro4u.improvements@gmail.com.
